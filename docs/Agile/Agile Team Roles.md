## Agile Team Roles

### 🤵 Product Owner (PO)

* Owns the **product vision and roadmap**.
* Manages and prioritizes the **product backlog**.
* Accepts or rejects completed user stories.
* The PO acts as the voice of the customer, ensuring the development team builds what provides the most value.

---

### 😎 Scrum Master

* Facilitates Agile ceremonies.
* Removes **impediments/blockers**.
* Ensures the team follows **Scrum practices**.
* Acts as a **servant leader** (not a manager).
* The Scrum Master also coaches the team on Agile principles and helps them improve their processes continuously.

---

### 😐 Business Analyst (BA)

* Translates business needs into **clear user stories**.
* Clarifies:
  * Business rules
  * Acceptance criteria
  * Edge cases
* In some teams, BA responsibilities are handled by the PO.
* BAs play a crucial role in bridging the gap between technical teams and business stakeholders, ensuring everyone has a shared understanding of requirements.

---

### 👨‍💻 Developers

* Design, develop, and unit-test the application.
* Responsible for delivering **working code** in each sprint.
* Developers collaborate closely with testers and other team members to ensure high-quality output.

---

### 🕺 QA / Test Team

* Validate user stories against acceptance criteria.
* Perform:
  * Functional testing
  * Regression testing
  * Automation testing
* Ensure quality before release.
* The QA team works alongside developers to catch defects early and provide feedback on usability and functionality.

---

### 👬 DevOps

* Manages:
  * CI/CD pipelines
  * Builds and deployments
  * Infrastructure and environments
* Enables **faster and reliable releases**.
* DevOps ensures that the software can be deployed efficiently and reliably, supporting continuous integration and delivery practices.
